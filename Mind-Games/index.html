We Get It

The challenges you’re already facing

Before we talk about what we offer, here’s what we’re here to solve.

🧠

Explaining What Mind Games Is

What you offer is truly unique, but it may not immediately resonate with audiences who have no idea who you are.

Our SolutionWith your help, we will create content that explains what Mind Games is in a relatable, fun, and easy-to-understand way. Utilising social media, short-form video content, and long-form SEO written content, we will help people understand what you do — saving you time on content creation.
🏃

Finding New Members

You’ve been creating content and reaching out to clubs, and we know it’s hard work.

Our SolutionWe will build a list of groups to conduct outreach to, saving you time and effort. This will include various sources across the internet, the NBRH network, PR publications, and local micro-influencers. Outreach will be conducted regularly to raise your profile as much as possible, saving you money on advertising.
🏢

Getting More Organisations and Businesses Onboard

We understand that you want to expand to businesses and other organisations for bespoke events, and we can help make that happen.

Our SolutionAs part of our outreach, we will contact small local businesses, corporate entities, amateur sports clubs, university sports groups, and local community groups. We will work with you to create bespoke proposals for these organisations, encouraging them to attend your sessions — saving you time and effort in the process.
🔁

Retaining New Members

Getting new members to give it a try is half the battle, the other half is getting them to come back.

Our SolutionWe will use user insights to understand what would encourage people to return, and apply those findings to improve the overall user journey. We will also explore customer retention strategies, such as loyalty programmes hosted on the NBRH, helping you retain members, add value to their experience, and open new channels of monetisation.

We’ll agree on your KPIs together — and if you want any changes, additions, or extra clarity, just say the word.

Here’s our promise.

We will push your club in front of more eyes and get the right people interested. We will be an extra pair of hands to support your growth. And we will use our platform to increase your digital capabilities.
