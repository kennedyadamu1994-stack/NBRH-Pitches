The real problem

Your sessions are great. Nobody outside your circle knows it.

The biggest challenge facing small sports clubs and exercise classes isn’t what happens inside the session — it’s what happens outside it. Getting people to find you, trust you, and show up for the first time is where most clubs get stuck.

The problem is ambiguity. Without a strong online presence, social proof, and consistent content, your club is a gamble to anyone who hasn’t been personally recommended you. Word of mouth only goes so far.

Word of mouth doesn’t scaleIt fills gaps sometimes, but it’s not a growth strategy. You can’t predict it, plan around it, or build a sustainable club on it.
You’re invisible where players searchWhen someone googles “5-a-side near me” or scrolls Instagram for a fitness class, you’re not showing up. The people who’d love your sessions never find them.
Cold drop-ins don’t come backPlayers who find you without context — no reviews, no story, no social proof — arrive as strangers and leave as strangers. You get one shot with a cold lead.
You don’t have time for marketingYou’re running sessions, managing admin, and keeping everything going. Marketing is the last thing on the list — and it shows.
