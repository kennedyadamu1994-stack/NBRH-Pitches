The Why

Running a club is harder than it looks.

You got into sport because you love it. But somewhere between coaching sessions, chasing payments, posting on Instagram and figuring out how to fill your next session — it stopped being fun. Sound familiar?

  • You don’t have the time
  • You don’t have the expertise
  • You don’t have the budget
  • Your sessions aren’t visible
  • You don’t know what to do
  • The admin never stops

We know this because we’ve lived it. Club House is what we wish had existed.

Club House is designed to give you back your time, amplify your reach, and provide the infrastructure to grow sustainably.

Consultative support

Branding, session formats, content direction, growth strategy — we’re a thinking partner, not just a tool. Available when you need guidance, not just when things go wrong.

Tools to scale and earn

Member subscriptions, the Neighbours Pass network, Club Store, and more. Everything is built to compound over time as The NBRH grows.

Coming Soon

The Neighbours Pass is coming.

We’re building a player-facing membership that gives your members more — and keeps them connected to your club. The Neighbours Pass is designed as a retention tool you can offer your players, tailored to your club, with access that stretches across the full NBRH network.

  • 🏭
    Built around your club Each club will be able to shape how the Pass looks and what it offers their members — their sessions, their perks, their own terms. Not a one-size-fits-all product.
  • 🔒
    A retention tool that actually works Once your members are on the Pass, their loyalty is tied to your club and to The NBRH. Their session history, preferences, and community all live on the platform.
  • 🌐
    One pass. Every NBRH venue. The Pass isn’t limited to one club. Members will be able to use it across every partner venue on The NBRH — giving them more value, and giving your club more pull.
  • 💲
    Offered to your members, not the public The Pass is something your club offers to its own players. We handle the infrastructure — you decide who gets access and how it’s positioned within your community.

What to Expect

Here’s what joining The NBRH looks like.

We won’t promise you the world. But here’s what we’re confident about — and what we’re accountable to delivering.

  • You’ll save time — bookings, content, admin, outreach all handled
  • More eyes on your sessions — platform discovery, Instagram, and outreach working at once
  • Proper digital infrastructure — booking system, Club Hub, analytics dashboard in one place
  • Clarity on what’s working — monthly reports with insights and clear next steps
  • A community behind your club — The NBRH network becomes your network
  • A foundation to scale — tools and support that compound over time

We don’t guarantee specific numbers — but we commit to showing up, doing the work, and being honest about what’s working.
