Ad spend is separate from your Club House fee and agreed with you before any campaign goes live. We handle the creative, targeting, and reporting — you decide the budget and approve the spend. There’s no hidden cost and no obligation to run paid ads if budget isn’t there right now.

We’d always aim for six months, but there’s no pressure after the initial term.

If your sessions are irregular or heavily seasonal, Club House Flex is probably the better fit — no monthly fee, just a small charge per booking when sessions are running. Talk to us and we’ll be honest about which makes more sense for your situation.
