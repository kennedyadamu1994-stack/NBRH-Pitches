The Why

Running a club is harder than it looks.

You got into sport because you love it. But somewhere between coaching sessions, chasing payments, posting on Instagram and figuring out how to fill your next session — it stopped being fun. Sound familiar?

  • You don’t have the time
  • You don’t have the expertise
  • You don’t have the budget
  • Your sessions aren’t visible
  • You don’t know what to do
  • The admin never stops

We know this because we’ve lived it. Club House is what we wish had existed.

The How

Here’s what we do about it.

Club House is designed to give you back your time, amplify your reach, and provide the infrastructure to grow sustainably.

📷

Expert-led marketing

Organic content, paid ads, SEO blog posts, LinkedIn articles, and in-person video — all created, managed and delivered by us. No briefings, no back-and-forth. We know your sessions, we know your audience.

🎫

Booking infrastructure

Your sessions are live on Tickts with a fully managed booking page. Events created, capacity managed, payments processed, waitlists handled. You just show up and coach.

📢

Community outreach

We reach out to Facebook Groups, local clubs, neighbourhoods, and relevant communities on your behalf — every week. Getting your sessions in front of the right people without you lifting a finger.

📉

Growth intelligence

Monthly analytics reports, traffic tracking, booking data, and AI-backed market research. You get clear insights and recommendations — not just numbers on a spreadsheet.

💬

Consultative support

Branding, session formats, content direction, growth strategy — we’re a thinking partner, not just a tool. Available when you need guidance, not just when things go wrong.

🚀

Tools to scale and earn

Member subscriptions, a people-finder tool, Club Store, and more. As The NBRH grows, so do your tools. Everything is built to compound over time.
