We Get It

The challenges you’re already facing

Before we talk about what we offer, here’s what we’re here to solve.

🏀

Constant supply of players to fill sessions

Sometimes, not all the sessions fill. And that can be really annoying, especially with a group chat with people that aren’t always active.

Our SolutionWe will use our marketing, network and outreach to find more players near east London, broadening the range of players that would be interested in your sessions. And if you’re ever looking for last minute players, we will reach out to our networks, social media and users to fill those last spots.
📋

Less booking admin

Having to run several sessions a week must be really time consuming. Having to track everything, write up all the sessions, handle all the comms. It must take so much time in your week.

Our SolutionWe’ll create a booking page & workflow that houses all of your sessions and handles bookings for weeks ahead. We’ll monitor these bookings, but you will have admin privileges. We’ll handle the grunt work to save you time each week. You tell us what to do, and we’ll get it done.
📉

Understanding my audience

Sometimes, it can be a bit of guesswork trying to understand why things are working or who is interested. Without the right tools from various channels, there’s no way to create a growth plan.

Our SolutionVia our booking tools, social tracking & platform tools, we can build a better picture of who is attending your sessions, who is interested and research to help you improve sessions. We’ll provide these findings to you in an easy and simple report. We can even run reports that you can use to attract future players.
🎫

Flexible pricing tools

You should be able to run & price sessions as you wish. Without having to create a new event.

Our SolutionWith our booking platform, we are able to create special discount codes that you can share with your players. Or give us the word and we can quickly change the prices of sessions. We can create different ticket types, bundles & even early bird tickets for your most keen players. We give you more control over your bookings.

We’ll agree on your KPIs together — and if you want any changes, additions, or extra clarity, just say the word.

Here’s our promise.

We will push your club in front of more eyes and get the right people interested. We will be an extra pair of hands to support your growth. And we will use our platform to increase your digital capabilities.
